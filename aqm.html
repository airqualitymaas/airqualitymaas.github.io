Air Quality Monitor

What is the air quality

The air quality monitor (AQM) was built using an arduino microcontroller and several environmental sensors. The different sensors measure CO2, particulate matter and ozone concentration and the amount of volatile organic compound (VOC) present in the air. Moreover, it can also measure the humidity and temperature of its surroundings. The concept of the AQM is mostly based on the How To Mechatronics project.(link). However, since we did not have access to all of the components some of them were changed for other parts. Before we could start building the AQM we had to test the function of the different sensors using simple Arduino circuits. The building process included building electronic circuits to connect the different sensors and coding them using various libraries to process the measurements.
